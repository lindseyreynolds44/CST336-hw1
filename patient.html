Patient Experience

AI is improving patients experience when going to the hospital.
Hospital staff can get overwhelmed by monotonous tasks that take away from their time helping patients. With AI, they can focus on walking patients through intake forms and quickly assisting them, shortening wait times.

Robots are being used to perform many types of surgeries, allowing doctors to have a magnified, clear view of the surgical site and more precision in their tasks.
