Robotics and AI in Health Care

Health care has seen some amazing advances with the progression of artificial intelligence and robotics. With sophisticated algorithms to diagnose disease or predict health outcomes, patients can have more accuracy and security in their medical care.
Hospital care can also be improved using AI. Care taking robots can now keep tabs on patients who may need quick response time if something goes wrong.
