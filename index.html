Healthcare and AI

Healthcare has seen some amazing advances with the innovation of artificial intelligence and robotics.
With sophisticated algorithms to diagnose disease or predict health outcomes, robots to improve both research and precision surgery, patients have more accuracy and security in their medical care.

AI has provided the medical field with:
  • The ability to analyze mass amounts of medical data in order to better care for patients
  • Improved patient experience through automation and robotics
  • Quick and inexpensive development and testing for new drugs
